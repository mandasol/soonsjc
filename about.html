Soon's Bakery and Cafe

About US

A KOREAN BAKERY CAFE WITH INTERNATIONAL INFLUENCE

Soon's Bakery was started using recipies and techniques from a very popular and well-known bakery in Seoul, Korea, and incorporating fresh ideas from Europe and the US. The original Soon’s Bakery first opened in Suwanee GA in April 2021. Two years later the second location opened in Johns Creek GA. Using all natural ingredients and no preservatives, Soon’s Bakery offers a wide variety of fresh breads, sandwiches, beverages and desserts everyday to the Georgia community.
